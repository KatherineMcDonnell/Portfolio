CONSULTING CASE STUDY

As a consultant with Students Consulting for Nonprofit Organizations (SCNO), I was tasked with creating a prioritization and scalability metric for a Chicago-based nonprofit to expand its services.

The Work

Task: Create a prioritization metric and expansion plan for an organization looking to expand its services.

Method: Census and demographics-by-industry data, broken down by county in Illinois.

Challenges: How do I develop criteria to capture the level of need for services in each of Illinois' 102 counties?

Insights: By creating a coded spreadsheet, I can ascribe weight to several variables to proide a prioritization output.

The Research

Using data and insights from databases such as Data USA, I was able to gather information on relevatn criteria including:

  • Industry Demographics
  • Population Density and Demographics
  • Population Breakdown by Languages Spoken

I gathered these data for each of Illinois' 102 counties.

The Final Product

After gathering the data, I created a coded spreadsheet to assign weights to each of the criteria. This allowed me to create a prioritization metric for the organization to use in its expansion plan.
