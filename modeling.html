MODELING 3-D CHARACTERS

What makes a character compelling? How can you tell a story through visuals alone?

Through my course on 3-D video game character modeling, I explored how the use of colors, accessories, and attention-grabbing traits can showcase the character of individuals and organizations alike..

Lo-Fi Character Creation

Task: Create a video game character in the style of games like Link's Awakening and Animal Crossing: New Horizons.

Method: Follow a process of initial concept design, to iterative models in Blender, to a final product.

Challenges: How do I bring a character's personality to life through visuals alone?

Insights: Through small details, like insignias, accessories, and 3-D assets, you can tell a vivid story through showing alone.

Step 1: Creating the concept

The first step in designing a compelling character is considering the colors, items, and detailing you want to use to show a story. Through platforms like PureRef, I created concept sheets that captured the intention I had with my character’s story and direction.

Step 2: Starting in Blender

After compiling the initial character concept, I moved into Blender to start making the general outline of my character's sillhouette.

Step 3: Iterative Modeling

After creating the general outline of my character, I focused in on specific features, like face shape, to bring further detail to the model.

Step 4: Finalizing the Model

The final step of the modeling process (before painting) comprised of using Blender's sculpting feature to make more detailed adjustments to the character's appearance.
